The Mario Amino Backup Project - a.k.a. AminoExporter

Any web service must come to an end some day - be it in a week or in a decade. With the popularity of the Amino social network slowly decreasing, an idea came to my mind.

Let's archive and preserve our content in this app, for when the developers shut Amino down, we'll have a backup!

I'm in the process of writing an easily usable and shareable program. It'll be hosted in my website soon.

If you're a fellow Amino user, I hope you are fine with this. This project is still in early development.
